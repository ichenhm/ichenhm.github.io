Biography

I’m currently a third-year Ph.D. student at Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University and a member of theory group. I’m advised by Professor Yitong Yin and Chaodong Zheng.

Before I joined Nanjing University, I obtained B.Sc. degree from Jilin University in June 2016, where I majored in Computer Science and Technology.

Research Interests

I’m interested in distributed algorithms, especially algorithms for wireless networks, and contention resolution problem.
